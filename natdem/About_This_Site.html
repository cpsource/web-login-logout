About This Web Site

This web site is dedicated toward helping America define and refine policy and platforms.

It's unfortunate, but the Democratic Party has lost site of its roots by trying to be more 'Republican' than the Republicans.

So, to get things back on track, this site focuses on Progressive Platforms for Working Americans.

It can be simply stated that this site represents the 'Democratic Wing' of the Democratic Party.

Progressive Platforms are not anti-business. Take for example, the issue of health care. Businesses are being burdened with ever rising costs, while at the same time, we have 41 million uninsured Americans. So, what's a solution that's mutually beneficial to both parties? One solution would reduce the costs to businesses, as well as provide at least some coverage for the uninsured. There is such a solution, and we propose it here in our web site.
